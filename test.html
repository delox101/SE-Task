One of the hardest things about improving your life is remembering to practice
what you've learned in a moment of temptation, frustration, or hardship. Anyone
can follow a strategy as they read about it, but remembering to stick with it in the
real world is tough.

Stories help with that. An engaging story sticks with you in a way that a research
finding often can't. While JamesClear.com promotes science-backed ideas, we
don't shun stories and lessons based on real life.

This page collects life lessons from my own life as well as from the lives of artists,
creators, and innovators. Articles grapple with concepts like marriage, gratitude,
and work-life balance.

Let's dive in.
